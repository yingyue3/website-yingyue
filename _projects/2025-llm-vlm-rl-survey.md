- Reviewed recent work that leverages LLMs and VLMs to enhance reinforcement learning, focusing on reward shaping, hierarchical planning, and multimodal decision-making.
- Developed a comprehensive taxonomy organizing existing literature into three categories: (1) LLM-assisted reward function design, (2) LLM-guided high-level planning, and (3) LLMs serving directly as RL agents.
- Analyzed strengths, limitations, and practical challenges of foundation-model-augmented RL, including interpretability, scalability, alignment, and computation.
- Outlined meaningful future directions such as improved multimodal grounding, adaptive prompting, and hybrid symbolic–neural approaches.


- Paper: [The Evolving Landscape of LLM- and VLM-Integrated Reinforcement Learning](https://doi.org/10.24963/ijcai.2025/1181)
