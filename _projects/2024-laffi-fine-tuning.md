- Engineered a system for incorporating natural language feedback during the fine-tuning of LLMs.
- Curated a dataset comprising around 1000 instances sourced from Squad datasets. Our dataset included questions and passages from Squad datasets, answers from the base model (LLaMa 7b) and feedback from human labeler.
- Finetuned the base model (LLaMa 7b) with human feedback.
- Developed experiments on size of LLM, data quality and data quantity.
- Constructed an evaluation pipeline to assess system performance.
- Paper: [LaFFi: Leveraging Hybrid Natural Language Feedback for Fine-tuning Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.00907)
