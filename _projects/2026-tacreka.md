- Lead the research of human-Centered LLM Design Reward Function                                                                            
- This research is at the intersection of Human-Centered AI, reinforcement learning, and large language models (LLMs), with a focus on developing reward-function design frameworks that more encode human values, intentions, and preferences. 
- Aimed to improve policy reliability and interpretability while reducing dependence on handcrafted or domain-specific reward signals. 
- Designed a Human-Centered AI pipeline that leverages LLMs to translate human preferences and implicit behavioral cues into structured reward-function components.
- Employed Evolutionary Search algorithms to explore the reward-function design space, enabling high-performing reward models with significantly fewer policy-training iterations.
