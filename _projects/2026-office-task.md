- Refined Vicuna-13b (an open-source chatbot from Meta AI) through development of finetuning pipelines.
- Constructed datasets highlighting household and office tasks with the assistance of Pangu, Huawei's Leading Language Model. This dataset aims to reconstruct the household and office tasks into machine executable small steps.
- Carried out annotations for the datasets.
